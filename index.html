My name is Caleb Mckay Anderson and I am a full time Front End Web Engineer for Cricut and part time freelance web designer/developer with the passion to build elegant websites. For the past 4 years I have sought out the knowledge of programming and the world wide web by attending Salt Lake Community College, researching online resources and learning academies to strengthen my abilities.

I build websites for small businesses that they can maintain with ease, builds their online presence, increases revenue to the brand and most importantly fits their budget. With the content management system (cms) you will be able to change any content on your site by editing any copy or images to keep your site looking great, all while saving money by not having to hire a full time web developer or designer.
